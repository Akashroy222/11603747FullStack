The first Story UnderConstruction Community, organized by Jerdan in 2017 was one of our efforts to improve diversity within the Art and Design community.

The Story UnderConstruction Is created by Jerdan. It is complemented by a web version, called StoryUC.com, created by P.Sami and N.Paro. To date, Story UnderConstruction are used by a worldwide community of artists, developer's, writer, designer, educators, students.

P.Sami
Lead Designer

P.Sami is an interdisciplinary artist and educator working at the intersection of technology, labor, and identity. P.Sami co-founded PSAM, intersectional feminist collective dedicated to women, She holds Bachelor’s degrees in Architecture Designer from the SDPS Women's collage, now she's the co-founder of the Story UnderConstruction. She initiated the School of Otherness which seeks to empower marginalized communities through storytelling, forums, and workshops that process experiences of the other. Their work has been exhibited at Ars Electronica, the Hammer Museum, Tiger Strikes Asteroid and Machine Project.
